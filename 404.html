ܠܐ ܬܫܦܠܘܢ ܒܐܓܘܢܐ ܡܠܐ ܢܨܚ̈ܢܐ. ܕܡܢܗ ܟܠ ܛܒ̈ܢ ܠܟܘܢ ܡ̈ܛܝܒܢ܀

Do not weaken in the contest that is full of feats of valour, for as a result of it all kind of good things have been prepared for you.
