How to Cite Syriaca.org Publications

Syriaca.org publishes a collection of digital reference resources that compile and classify core data for the study of Syriac sources, including persons, places, works, and secondary scholarship. Preferred citations for individual records are provided on their web pages, and include information about the contributors to that record. To cite a Syriaca.org publication as a whole, however, please use the following citation formats. These formats derive from the Chicago Manual of Style and include both 'notes' and 'bibliography' formats. They may be adapted for other citations styles as needed.
