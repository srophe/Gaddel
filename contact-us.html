Contact Us

Syriaca.org is designed by and for the Syriac Studies scholarly community. We invite you to join the project as a collaborator. If you have comments, feedback, or would like to suggest a particular avenue of collaboration, please contact the general editor, David Michelson, at info@syriaca.org.

We are also grateful to all financial supporters of our work. Financial contributions can be made through Vanderbilt University at www.vu.edu/syriac or by contacting info@syriaca.org.
